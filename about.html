About the project

A digital library for technical education

The goal is to make useful study materials easier to find for technical college students throughout Malawi.

Our purpose

Malawi Technical Library organises books, notes, pamphlets, modules, practical guides and past papers in one accessible place.

Copyright approach

Commercial books should not be uploaded without permission. The platform should prioritise original notes, public resources, authorised papers and open educational materials.

A reporting and removal process should be added before public launch.
